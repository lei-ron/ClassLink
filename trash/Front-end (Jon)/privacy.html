How we handle your data

Please read carefully

1. Information We Collect

When you use our service we collect different types of information:

  • Personal information: Name, email address, school/organization affiliation, and login credentials
  • Educational Content: Files, messages, notes, and collaborative materials you create or share with the platform
  • Usage Information: IP address, device information, operating system and browser type, access times, pages viewed, and referring website addresses
  • Stored Content: Files, messages, notes, and collaborative materials you upload or create within the platform

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide you with our educational collaboration platform
  • Process your account registration and manage your account
  • Improve user experience and optimize platform performance
  • Ensure compliance with educational standards and legal requirements
  • Send notifications about platform updates, security alerts, and administrative messages

3. Data Sharing and Access

We limit how we share your information:

  • Within Your Organization: Teachers, supervisors, or administrators may access content you share or collaborative spaces
  • Service Providers: Trusted third party providers may help us operate, provide, or improve our service. They are contractually obligated to protect your information
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect low rights and safety of users

4. Data Storage and Security

We protect your information with:

  • All information is stored on secure servers with encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and two-factor authentication where applicable
  • Incident response plans and monitoring for suspected or protect against unauthorized access
  • Access to sensitive data is restricted to authorized personnel who need it for legitimate business purposes

5. User Responsibilities

Keep your login credentials confidential

  • Alert us if you suspect anyone has gained unauthorized access that complies with our community guidelines
  • Report any suspected unauthorized access to your account
  • Use the platform in a way that respects others' privacy and rights

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Request a copy of the information we have about you
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (which may affect your use of the platform)

7. Updates to This Policy

We retain your information only as long as necessary for educational and operational purposes as required by law. If we make substantial changes to this policy we may notify users about changes to privacy practices but we cannot guarantee we will be notified

  • We may update these terms to reflect new features, legal requirements, or to clarify practices. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date
